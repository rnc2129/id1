Richard N. Cooke is a native Californian whom has been transplanted in the fertile soils of Virginia Beach, Virginia.

Studying Studio Arts along side with Graphic Design has really helped him develop his craft and trade.

Blending an artists eye with attention to detail he has carved out his own unique style. Clean and innovative with a nostalgic flare.

"I call upon all my experiences to help guide my work, if I can paint or build it, I can create it on the screen.

- Richard N. Cooke Jr.
